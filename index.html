Who can participate in the AI Championship?

The competition is open to university students enrolled in undergraduate or graduate programs from both Pakistan and international institutions.

How do I register for the Championship?

All participants (domestic and international) must register through our official website by filling out the application form before the deadline.

Is there a registration fee?

No, registration is completely free for all participants.

Can I register as an individual or do I need a team?

Only team registrations are accepted. Each team must have 24 members. Cross-border teams are welcome!

Will international teams participate virtually or on-site?

International teams will participate virtually in the initial rounds. Shortlisted finalists may be invited to join the grand finale either virtually or in-person, depending on travel and visa arrangements.

What is the format of the competition?

The Championship includes idea submission, MVP (prototype) submission, mentorship, and a final pitch round judged by industry experts.

Will mentorship be provided to international teams as well?

Yes, all shortlisted teams local and international will receive remote mentorship from our pool of industry experts.

What kind of AI projects are eligible?

We welcome AI-driven solutions addressing real-world challenges in areas like health, education, sustainability, fintech, agriculture, and beyond.

Are the rewards the same for international participants?

Yes, recognition and prizes are awarded equally, regardless of geographic location. International winners will be recognized with digital awards and global exposure.

How can I stay updated on key dates and announcements?

All updates will be posted on our website and shared through our official social media platforms. You can also subscribe to our newsletter for regular updates.
