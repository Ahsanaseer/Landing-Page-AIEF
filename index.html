Who can participate in the AI Championship?

The competition is open to university students enrolled in undergraduate or graduate programs from both Pakistan and international institutions.

Learn more about AIEF

How do I register for the Championship?

Registration can be completed online through our official website. Simply fill out the registration form with your team details and project information. Early registration is recommended as spots are limited.

Register Now

Is there a registration fee?

The registration fee is Rs. 5,000 per team. This includes participation in the championship, access to all events, and competition materials. Payment can be made online through our secure payment gateway.

View Fee Details

Can I register as an individual or do I need a team?

Teams can consist of 2-4 members. Individual registrations are not accepted. We encourage collaboration and diverse skill sets within teams to create innovative AI solutions.

Team Guidelines

Will international teams participate virtually or on-site?

International teams have the option to participate either virtually or on-site. Virtual participation includes live streaming of presentations and remote judging. On-site participation provides the full championship experience.

International Guidelines

What are the project submission categories?

We offer multiple categories including Business Analytics, AI in Agriculture, Intelligent App Creation, AI in Fintech, AI in Urban Tech, AI in Retail & E-commerce, AI in Robotics, and AI in Healthcare. Choose the category that best fits your project.

View All Categories

What are the cash prizes for winners?

The championship offers substantial cash prizes: 1st Prize - Rs. 300,000, 2nd Prize - Rs. 200,000, and 3rd Prize - Rs. 100,000. Additionally, winners receive recognition and networking opportunities.

Prize Details

What is the competition schedule?

Registration opens now, project submission deadline is September 7, 2025, screening completes by September 19, shortlisted participants announced by September 25, and the final championship event is on September 27, 2025.

Full Schedule

What technical requirements are needed?

Teams need to submit a working AI solution with documentation, presentation materials, and a demo video. Technical infrastructure will be provided on-site, but teams should bring their own laptops for presentations.

Technical Guidelines
